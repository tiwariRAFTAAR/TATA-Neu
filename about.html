People involved in building Tata Neu have echoed Chandrasekaran's involvement in the project. “Not just Mukesh and Pratik, but he has been involved with new-age founders and other key executives at Tata Digital while building out the Tata Neu up. The newly-launched Tata Neu app, which houses major Tata Group brands from Tata Croma to BigBasket, will be open to brands outside of the group, executive Chairman N Chandrasekaran indicated while addressing a press conference to mark the launch of the app. “Our aim is to ensure that Neu becomes a super app which simplifies the life of every consumer from a product, services and financial needs point of view. And for that the offerings may come from the Tata table or it may come from outside,” he added. Tata Digital had launched the much-awaited Neu app last week. The app houses most Tata brands except for Vistara, Air India, Titan, Tanishq, Tata Motors, which are expected to join the platform soon.


The app, according to the company's top management, has witnessed a pheonomenal response and has been downloaded 2.2 million times.
